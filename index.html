Welcome!

Welcome to the Retro Gaming Zone! Not many know that the retro gaming community has a large collection of information of older and even obsucure games that the general public doesnt know much about as well as a large "economy" where many retro games are bought and sold either in brick and mortar stores, in flea markets or garage sales and definitely online. This community is fueled by nostalgia and the wish to get to play the games they never played as a child. Anyone who is a gaming enthusaist or just getting into retro gaming and want to learn more about retro games you can find information on retro games here.
